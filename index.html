Service Above Self

Discover the global movement of Rotary and how our local club brings that mission to life right here in the historic city of Bath.

A Global Network of Neighbours

Rotary International is a global network of 1.2 million leaders and problem-solvers who see a world where people unite and take action to create lasting change. For more than 115 years, Rotary members have used their passion and energy to tackle sustainable projects, from promoting peace and fighting disease to providing clean water and supporting education.

Our Local Impact in Bath, UK

The Rotary Club of Bath is a vibrant group of people from this historic city and its surrounding area who work together to make Bath, and the wider world, a better place. We organize fundraising events, support local charities with our time and skills, and partner on major humanitarian projects. Our focus is on creating positive, lasting change for our community.

Get Involved & Make a Difference

Becoming a Rotarian connects you with a diverse group of professionals who share your drive to give back. It's an opportunity for personal and professional growth, networking, and making a real impact. Whether you join as a member, a friend of Rotary, or a project volunteer, your contribution is valued.
